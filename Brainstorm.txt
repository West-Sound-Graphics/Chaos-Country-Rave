retro‑inspired arcade action‑puzzle
top‑down digging, bubble‑inflating enemies, and rock‑falling tactics

# Core Gameplay Loop
    Step: Dig 
    What Happens: The player moves the drill left/right/up/down, carving new paths through layers of soil.
    Player Goal: Create a safe corridor or trap for enemies.

    Step: Encounter
    What Happens: Enemies (Pooka‑style “Bubbles” and Fuzzy‑style “Goblins”) appear in the tunnel.
    Player Goal: Survive their approach.

    Step: Attack
    What Happens: 1. Inflate enemies with the air cannon to pop them. 2. Drop rocks from above by breaking the ceiling.
    Player Goal: Eliminate all foes before they reach you or your base.

    Step: Collect & Upgrade
    What Happens: Pick up coins, power‑ups, and upgrade tokens scattered in the tunnel.
    Player Goal: Improve drill speed, cannon range, rock durability, etc.

    Step: Repeat
    What Happens: New enemies spawn as the player continues to dig deeper.
    Player Goal: Clear each level while managing resources and avoiding traps.

# Enemies & Challenges
    Bubble‑Busters (Pookas): Inflate them until they burst; otherwise, they’ll explode on contact with you or the walls.
    Gloom‑Goblins (Fuzzies): They move in straight lines and can be crushed by falling rocks.
    Magma Pits & Spike Traps: Static hazards that require careful planning to avoid.
    Boss Encounters: At level milestones, a giant “Earth Serpent” challenges the player’s timing and strategy.

# Power‑Ups & Upgrades
    Item: Turbo Drill
    Effect: Increases digging speed temporarily.

    Item: Super Air Cannon
    Effect: Inflates enemies faster, handles larger bubbles.

    Item: Stone Skin
    Effect: Grants temporary immunity to falling rocks.

    Item: Coin Magnet
    Effect: Pulls nearby coins toward the player for a short burst.

    Item: Upgrade Tokens
    Effect: Unlock new drill skins, cannon colors, and bonus lives.

# Visual & Audio Style
    Pixel‑Art Graphics: 6×16 pixel sprites with vibrant color palettes reminiscent of early ’80s arcade cabinets.
    Dynamic Lighting: Subtle shading to emphasize depth in the underground layers.
    Chiptune Soundtrack: Energetic, looping tracks that drive the pace and shift when a boss appears.
