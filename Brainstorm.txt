# Game: Chaos Country Rave
# Tagline: “Dig, perform, crush—turn every crowd into a glitter‑filled ovation!”

# Game Overview
    top‑down arcade action game that takes the core loop of Dig Dug and pumps it up with Miley Cyrus‑style flair: neon lights, flashy outfits, anthemic pop beats, and an empowering message about turning negativity into applause.
    Setting: A series of ever‑evolving concert stages (from a neon‑lit club in Los Angeles to a massive stadium in Rio).
    Goal: Clear each stage of “noise” (critics, trolls, bored fans) while collecting chart‑topping hits and backstage props.

    retro‑inspired arcade action‑puzzle
    top‑down digging, bubble‑inflating enemies, and rock‑falling tactics



# Core Gameplay Loop
    Step: Dig 
    What Happens: You move a mic‑drill left/right/up/down through layers of stage dust and sound‑waves.
    Player Goal: Create safe paths, uncover secret props

    Step: Encounter
    What Happens: Enemies (Pooka‑style “Bubbles” and Fuzzy‑style “Goblins”) appear in the tunnel.
    Player Goal: Survive their approach.

    Step: Attack
    What Happens: 1. Mic Blast: Blow enemies into glittering confetti. 2. Drop Stage Props: Release a falling cymbal or drumstick that crushes them from above.
    Player Goal: Eliminate all foes before they reach you.

    Step: Collect & Upgrade
    What Happens: Pick up coins, power‑ups, and upgrade tokens scattered in the tunnel.
    Player Goal: Improve drill speed, cannon range, rock durability, etc.

    Step: Repeat
    What Happens: New enemies spawn as the player continues to dig deeper.
    Player Goal: Clear each level while managing resources and avoiding traps.

# Characters
    Pop Star (Player) A stylized, neon‑clad avatar inspired by Miley’s iconic looks (glittered bodysuit). Controls the mic‑drill; can perform dance moves to gain temporary speed. 
    DJ‑Drone (Boss) A hovering DJ booth with spinning turntables. Fires spinning record blasts and summons a swarm of “Microphone Bubbles.” Defeating it triggers a full‑stage encore—bonus points and a new song unlock.

# Enemies & Challenges
    Bubble‑Busters (Pookas): Inflate them until they burst; otherwise, they’ll explode on contact with you or the walls.
    Gloom‑Goblins (Fuzzies): They move in straight lines and can be crushed by falling rocks.
    Magma Pits & Spike Traps: Static hazards that require careful planning to avoid.
    Boss Encounters: At level milestones, a giant “Earth Serpent” challenges the player’s timing and strategy.

# Power‑Ups & Upgrades
    Item: Glow‑Mic
    Effect: Increases blast radius; adds a sparkling trail.

    Item: Encore Shot
    Effect: Fires a rapid volley of confetti blasts for a short burst.

    Item: Stage Shield
    Effect: Temporary invincibility that shimmers like a hologram.

    Item: Prop Pack
    Effect: Drops a stack of props (T‑shirts, hats) that grant extra points.

    Item: Beat Booster
    Effect: Temporarily speeds up music tempo, boosting your drill speed.

# Visual & Audio Style
    Pixel‑Art Graphics: 6×16 pixel sprites with vibrant color palettes reminiscent of early ’80s arcade cabinets.
    Dynamic Lighting: Subtle shading to emphasize depth in the underground layers.
    Chiptune Soundtrack: Energetic, looping tracks that drive the pace and shift when a boss appears.
    Animations: Smooth dance moves for the player; bubbles pop with glitter effects; falling props leave a sparkling trail.
